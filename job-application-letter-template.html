What Is a Job Application Letter Template?

A job application letter is a formal, standalone letter used to apply directly for a role, often referencing exactly where you saw the opening and summarizing your qualifications and availability. It's more formal in structure than a typical cover letter and can stand on its own.

How to Use This Template

  1. Fill in the fields on the left — your entries appear in the live preview.
  2. Click "Update Preview" any time you change a field.
  3. Click directly inside the preview to fine-tune wording, spacing, or line breaks.
  4. Download as a text file, or use Print → Save as PDF for a polished document.

Tips

  • Name exactly where you saw the job posting — it shows attentiveness and helps the employer track sources.
  • Summarize your top 2-3 qualifications directly against what the posting asks for.
  • State your availability to start clearly near the end.
  • Mention that your resume and references are available or attached.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from the Cover Letter Template?

A cover letter typically accompanies a resume and focuses on fit and enthusiasm for a specific opening. This job application letter is a more formal, standalone letter that explicitly references the job posting source and can be used with or without a separate resume.

Should I attach my resume with this letter?

Yes, if you have one — mention it's attached in the letter. This template can also stand alone if a formal resume isn't required.

Do I need to reference where I saw the job?

It's good practice — it shows you're responding to a specific opening rather than sending a generic application.

Can I download this to submit to an employer?

Yes, use Print → Save as PDF for a formatted document, or download as .txt to paste into an application portal.
