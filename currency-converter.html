Convert Currencies With Live Exchange Rates

This currency converter fetches current exchange rates and converts between 29 major world currencies, including USD, EUR, GBP, PKR, INR, AUD, CAD, and more. Enter an amount, choose your source and target currency, and see the converted value instantly.

Rates are fetched from a public exchange-rate service and cached in your browser for a few hours to keep things fast. If a live rate can't be fetched (for example, if you're offline), the tool falls back to a stored estimate and clearly labels it as such, so you always know whether you're looking at live or estimated figures.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these exchange rates live?

Yes, when your browser can reach the exchange-rate service, rates are fetched live and cached for up to six hours to keep the tool fast.

What happens if the live rate can't be fetched?

The converter falls back to a stored estimated rate table and clearly labels the result as an estimate rather than a live rate.

How often do the rates update?

Cached rates refresh automatically after six hours, or whenever your browser's cache is cleared.

Which currencies are supported?

29 major currencies are supported, including USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, PKR, INR, AUD, CAD, CHF, CNY, AED, SAR, and more, listed in the dropdown menus.

Is this suitable for large financial transactions?

This tool is meant for quick, informal reference. For time-sensitive or high-value transactions, check rates directly with your bank or a licensed currency exchange.
