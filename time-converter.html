Frequently Asked Questions

How many minutes are in a day?

There are 1,440 minutes in a day (24 hours × 60 minutes).

How is a month calculated in this converter?

A month is approximated as 30.44 days (the average length of a calendar month across a year), since months vary from 28 to 31 days.

How is a year calculated?

A year is approximated as 365.25 days to account for leap years. For calendar-specific date math, try the Date Calculator.

Can I convert seconds to hours?

Yes, select seconds as the source unit and hours as the target for an instant, accurate result.

Is this useful for converting video or audio durations?

Yes, it works well for converting milliseconds or seconds into minutes and hours for media or technical timing needs.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, completely free with no sign-up or account required.
