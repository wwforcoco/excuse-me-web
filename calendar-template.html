What Is a Calendar Template?

This template generates a full monthly calendar grid for any month and year you choose, with optional events dropped into the right days. It's built for printing or downloading a blank or lightly annotated month at a glance.

How to Use This Template

  1. Fill in the fields on the left — your entries appear in the live preview.
  2. Click "Update Preview" any time you change a field.
  3. Click directly inside the preview to fine-tune wording, spacing, or line breaks.
  4. Download as a text file, or use Print → Save as PDF for a polished document.

Tips

  • Add events using the format 'Day: Event text' — one per line — and they'll appear in the matching date cell.
  • Generate a blank calendar by leaving the events field empty — handy as a printable planning sheet.
  • Use Print → Save as PDF for the cleanest printable version of the grid.
  • Regenerate the preview after changing the month or year to rebuild the grid correctly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I generate a calendar for any month and year?

Yes, choose any month and year and the grid rebuilds to match, including the correct starting weekday and number of days.

How do I add events to specific days?

List them in the events field as 'Day: Event', one per line — for example '4: Team offsite'.

How is this different from the Schedule Template?

This calendar is a full monthly grid, useful for a big-picture view of the month. The Schedule Template is a weekly, time-blocked list better suited to hour-by-hour planning.

Can I print a blank calendar to fill in by hand?

Yes, leave the events field empty and print the grid — it works as a blank planning sheet.
