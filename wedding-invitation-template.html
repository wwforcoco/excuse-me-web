What Is a Wedding Invitation Template?

A wedding invitation formally announces a wedding and gives guests the details they need to attend — the couple's names, date, time, venue, and how to RSVP. This template uses an elegant, classic layout suited specifically to weddings.

How to Use This Template

  1. Fill in the fields on the left — your entries appear in the live preview.
  2. Click "Update Preview" any time you change a field.
  3. Click directly inside the preview to fine-tune wording, spacing, or line breaks.
  4. Download as a text file, or use Print → Save as PDF for a polished document.

Tips

  • Traditionally, invitations go out six to eight weeks before the wedding — earlier for destination weddings.
  • Spell out the date and time in full for a formal feel ('the twelfth of September' vs '9/12').
  • Include a clear RSVP deadline, typically three to four weeks before the wedding.
  • Mention dress code only if it's not the assumed default for the venue and time of day.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from the general Invitation Template?

This template is written specifically for weddings, with fields for both partners' names and more formal, traditional wording. Use the general Invitation Template for birthdays, gatherings, or other events.

How far in advance should wedding invitations go out?

Six to eight weeks before the wedding is standard, with save-the-dates sent even earlier if needed.

Can I include a dress code or additional notes?

Yes, there's a field for additional notes where you can add dress code, gift registry info, or other details.

Can I download or print this invitation?

Yes, use Print → Save as PDF for a polished, printable copy, or download as .txt to include in a digital invite.
