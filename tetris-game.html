What Is Tetris Game?

This Tetris-style game drops seven classic block shapes, one at a time, down a 10-wide board. Move and rotate each falling piece to fit it against the stack, and clear points by completing full horizontal rows before the stack reaches the top.

How to Play

  • Move the falling piece left or right and rotate it to fit gaps in the stack
  • Complete a full horizontal row to clear it and score points
  • Clearing multiple rows at once earns a larger score bonus
  • The drop speed increases as your line count grows, so plan placements quickly

Strategy Tips

  • Keep the stack flat and avoid leaving gaps that are hard to fill later
  • Save the long straight piece for clearing multiple lines at once when possible
  • Watch the Next preview so you can plan your current placement around it
  • Use soft drop for control and hard drop only when you are confident in the placement

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I control the falling blocks?

Use the left and right arrow keys to move, the up arrow to rotate, the down arrow to soft drop, and space bar to hard drop. On mobile, use the on-screen arrow buttons or swipe and tap on the board.

How does scoring work?

Points are awarded for soft and hard drops and, most importantly, for clearing full horizontal lines, with bigger bonuses for clearing multiple lines at once.

Does the game get faster over time?

Yes, blocks fall progressively faster as you clear more lines, increasing the challenge as your line count grows.

What ends the game?

The game ends when a new piece has no room to appear at the top of the board, meaning the stack has reached the top.
