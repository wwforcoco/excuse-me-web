What Is a Percentage Calculator?

A percentage calculator finds what one number represents as a fraction of 100, so you can calculate percentage of a number, percentage increase, percentage decrease, or the percent difference between two values in one place.

How to Calculate Percentage

  • To find a percentage of a number, multiply the number by the percentage, then divide by 100
  • To find what percent one number is of another, divide the part by the whole, then multiply by 100
  • To find a percentage increase or decrease, use the calculator above and enter your original and new values

Percentage Formula

  • Percentage: (X ÷ 100) × Y = result
  • Percent of total: (X ÷ Y) × 100 = percent
  • Percentage change: ((New − Original) ÷ Original) × 100 = percent change

Common Percentage Examples

  • 20% of 50 is 10
  • 15 is 30% of 50
  • A price moving from $80 to $100 is a 25% increase
  • A price moving from $100 to $80 is a 20% decrease
  • A 20% tip on a $60 bill is $12

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate a percentage increase?

Subtract the original value from the new value, divide by the original, then multiply by 100.

What does "X is what percent of Y" mean?

It determines the proportional relationship where X represents a fraction of Y, expressed on a 100-point scale.

Can this tool handle negative percentages?

Yes. Negative results indicate a decrease, reduction, or loss relative to the starting value.

How do I find the original price before a discount?

Divide the sale price by (1 - discount percentage as a decimal).

What is the difference between percentage points and percent change?

Percentage points measure absolute difference (5% to 7% = 2 points), while percent change measures relative growth (5% to 7% = 40% increase).
