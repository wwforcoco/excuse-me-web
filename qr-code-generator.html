QR Code Generator

Turn any link or text into a downloadable QR code.

About This Tool

This tool builds a QR code directly in your browser from whatever text or link you enter, then lets you download it as a PNG image to print or share.

How It Works

  1. Type or paste the text, link, or message you want encoded.
  2. Choose an output size.
  3. Click Generate QR Code to preview it.
  4. Click Download PNG to save the image.

What This Tool Covers

  • Works with URLs, plain text, or short messages
  • Adjustable output size from 180px to 400px
  • Instant PNG download
  • No limit on how many codes you generate

Frequently Asked Questions

What can I put into a QR code?

Any text works, including website links, Wi-Fi details formatted as text, or a short message.

Do QR codes generated here expire?

No, a QR code just encodes the text you gave it; it doesn't expire or depend on this site staying online.

Can I print the QR code?

Yes, download the PNG and it will print clearly at most common sizes.

Is my text sent to a server to build the code?

No, the QR code is drawn locally in your browser using JavaScript.
