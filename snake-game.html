What Is Snake Game?

Snake is a classic arcade game where you guide a constantly moving line around a grid, picking up food to grow longer while avoiding the walls and your own tail. Each piece of food eaten adds to your score and to the snake's length, which makes navigating the board progressively harder.

How to Play

  • Move the snake using the arrow keys, WASD, swipes, or the on-screen arrows
  • Guide the snake's head into the orange dot to eat and grow
  • Avoid crossing the board edges or the snake's own body
  • Pick a speed before starting, then try to beat your best score

Strategy Tips

  • Leave yourself an escape route rather than boxing the snake into a corner
  • Loop around the open edges of the board instead of cutting through the center early
  • Slow down mentally as the snake gets longer since the safe space keeps shrinking
  • Practice at a slower speed first, then increase it once turns feel natural

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I control the snake?

Use the arrow keys or WASD on desktop, swipe on the canvas on a touchscreen, or tap the on-screen directional pad shown on mobile.

What ends the game?

The game ends if the snake runs into a wall or crosses over its own body.

Can I change the speed?

Yes, use the Slow, Normal, or Fast buttons above the board to change how quickly the snake moves before starting a new attempt.

Is my best score kept?

Your best score is saved in your browser's local storage, so it persists between visits without needing an account.
