What Is Tic Tac Toe?

Tic Tac Toe is a two-player strategy game played on a 3x3 grid. Players take turns placing their mark, X or O, and the first to line up three marks in a row, column, or diagonal wins the round.

How to Play

  • Choose 2 Players to play locally with a friend, or Vs Computer to play against the built-in opponent
  • Tap any empty square to place your mark; X always moves first
  • Line up three matching marks in a row, column, or diagonal to win
  • Start New Round to play again, or Reset Scores to clear the running tally

Strategy Tips

  • Taking the center square early gives you the most possible winning lines
  • Watch for two-in-a-row threats from your opponent and block them before completing your own
  • Corners are generally stronger opening moves than edge squares
  • Against the computer, a perfect opening from the center usually leads to a draw at best

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Vs Computer mode work?

The computer plays as O and always makes the strongest available move, so a perfectly played game against it will end in a draw.

Can two people play on the same device?

Yes, switch to 2 Players mode and take turns tapping the board on the same screen, with X always going first.

How do I win a round?

Get three of your marks in a row, horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, before your opponent does.

Does the game keep score across rounds?

Yes, wins for X, wins for O, and draws are tracked above the board until you press Reset Scores.
