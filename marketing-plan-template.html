What Is a Marketing Plan Template?

A marketing plan lays out how you'll reach a target audience — which channels you'll use, what you'll say, how much you'll spend, and how you'll know it worked. This template breaks that down into audience, goals, channel strategy, and KPIs.

How to Use This Template

  1. Fill in the fields on the left — your entries appear in the live preview.
  2. Click "Update Preview" any time you change a field.
  3. Click directly inside the preview to fine-tune wording, spacing, or line breaks.
  4. Download as a text file, or use Print → Save as PDF for a polished document.

Tips

  • Define your target audience narrowly — specific messaging outperforms generic messaging.
  • Set goals that are measurable (leads, sign-ups, revenue), not just 'raise awareness'.
  • Assign a rough budget to each channel so spend is intentional, not reactive.
  • Pick 2-4 KPIs to track closely rather than trying to measure everything.
  • Revisit the plan monthly and adjust channels that aren't performing.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from the Business Plan Template?

The Business Plan covers your whole business — mission, product, market, finances. This Marketing Plan focuses specifically on channels, tactics, budget, and KPIs for a marketing effort or campaign.

Can I list a budget per channel?

Yes, enter each strategy as Channel | Tactic | Budget and it renders as a structured table.

Is this suitable for a single campaign or an ongoing plan?

Both — use it for a single campaign push or as a recurring quarterly marketing plan.

Can I download this to share with my team?

Yes, download as .txt or use Print → Save as PDF for a formatted document.
