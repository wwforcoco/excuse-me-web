Frequently Asked Questions

How many pounds are in a kilogram?

One kilogram equals approximately 2.20462 pounds.

What is a stone in weight measurement?

A stone is a British imperial unit equal to 14 pounds, commonly used to state body weight in the UK and Ireland.

Is a US ton the same as a metric ton?

No. A US (short) ton is 907.18474 kg, while a metric ton is exactly 1,000 kg. The converter treats them as separate units.

Can I convert grams to ounces for a recipe?

Yes, select grams as the source unit and ounces as the target to get an instant, accurate conversion.

Does this handle very small weights like milligrams?

Yes, milligrams are included, useful for precise measurements like medication dosages or lab work.

Do I need to sign up to use this converter?

No sign-up, account, or payment is required — it's free to use directly in your browser.
