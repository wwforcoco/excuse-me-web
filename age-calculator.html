What Is an Age Calculator?

An age calculator helps you find your exact age from your date of birth. It calculates your age in years, months, days, hours, minutes, and seconds.

Common Uses

  • Verifying eligibility for age-restricted services and programs
  • Planning milestone birthday celebrations and retirement dates
  • Completing medical forms requiring exact patient age
  • Calculating senior citizen benefit qualification thresholds

How It Works

  • Select your date of birth from the calendar picker
  • The calculator compares it with today's date
  • Your age is displayed instantly in years, months, days, hours, minutes and seconds
  • Birthday countdown updates automatically

Pro Tips

  • For genealogy research, enter historical dates to determine ancestor ages at specific events
  • The months count includes partial months, not just completed full months
  • Compare two peoples ages to find exact age gaps in days
  • The birthday countdown resets automatically after each celebration passes

Frequently Asked Questions

How does this tool handle leap years?

February 29 births calculate correctly, with age incremented on February 28 in non-leap years or March 1 depending on preference.

Can I calculate age for someone who has passed away?

Yes. Enter the birth date and the date of passing to determine exact lifespan duration.

What time zone does the countdown use?

All calculations reference your device's local system clock.

Does the calculator account for different calendar systems?

Currently operates on the Gregorian calendar standard used internationally.

How accurate is the seconds display?

It reflects the precise interval between your birth timestamp and the current moment, updating each second.
