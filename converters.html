Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the results, and how many decimal places should I trust?

Conversions use precise, high-decimal factors, but the number of digits worth trusting depends on your original measurement. A value measured to two significant figures shouldn't be reported to six decimal places just because the tool displays them.

Is my data sent to a server?

No. Every conversion, aside from live currency rates, runs directly in your browser, and nothing you type is stored or transmitted.

Are kilograms and pounds the same kind of unit?

Not technically. Kilograms measure mass, while pounds are traditionally a unit of weight, which depends on gravity. For everyday, Earth-bound use, this converter treats them as interchangeable.

Which converter should I use for everyday unit conversions?

The Unit Converter covers 14 categories in one page. For a focused, single-purpose tool, use the dedicated converter for that category instead.

Does the Currency Converter use real-time rates?

It fetches live exchange rates when possible and falls back to a clearly labeled estimate if a live rate can't be reached.
