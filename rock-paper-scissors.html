What Is Rock Paper Scissors?

Rock Paper Scissors is a quick hand-game where two players simultaneously choose one of three options. Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock, with matching choices ending in a draw that gets replayed.

How to Play

  • Pick a match length: a single round, best of 3, or best of 5
  • Tap rock, paper, or scissors to lock in your choice for the round
  • The computer reveals its choice at the same time and the result is scored instantly
  • First to reach the required number of wins takes the match

Strategy Tips

  • Since the computer's pick is random, there's no pattern to memorize, so any choice is equally likely to win
  • Try a best of 5 match if you want a longer back-and-forth instead of a single round decision
  • Reset the match at any point if you want a clean scoreboard for a fresh set of rounds

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the rules?

Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock. Matching choices result in a draw and the round is replayed.

Can I play more than one round?

Yes, switch to Best of 3 or Best of 5 above the game to play a short match instead of a single round.

Is the computer's choice random?

Yes, the computer picks rock, paper, or scissors at random for every round, with no pattern to exploit.

How do I start a new match?

Press Reset Match to clear the score and begin again at your selected match length.
