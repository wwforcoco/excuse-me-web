Frequently Asked Questions

How many watts are in one horsepower?

One mechanical horsepower equals approximately 745.7 watts.

What's the difference between mechanical and metric horsepower?

Mechanical (imperial) horsepower is 745.7 watts, while metric horsepower (PS) is slightly less at 735.5 watts; both are included separately.

Can I convert kilowatts to horsepower for a car engine?

Yes, select kilowatt as the source unit and mechanical horsepower as the target for an instant conversion.

What is BTU per hour used for?

BTU per hour is a common way to rate heating and cooling equipment power output, especially in the US.

Is this converter accurate for electrical appliance wattage?

Yes, watts and kilowatts convert using standard SI definitions suitable for electrical appliances.

Do I need to sign up to use this tool?
