What Is 2048?

2048 is a single-player sliding tile puzzle played on a 4x4 grid. Every move slides all tiles as far as possible in the chosen direction, and any two tiles that touch with the same number combine into one tile with double the value. The game adds a new tile after each move, so the challenge is managing space while chasing bigger numbers.

How to Play

  • Press an arrow key, or swipe on a touchscreen, to slide every tile in that direction
  • Matching tiles that collide merge into a single tile worth their combined value
  • A new tile (2 or 4) appears in an empty spot after each successful move
  • The round ends when the grid is full and no merges are possible

Strategy Tips

  • Keep your highest tile anchored in one corner instead of chasing merges across the board
  • Build in one general direction so tiles stack predictably instead of scattering
  • Avoid moves that only shuffle tiles without creating a merge when possible
  • Plan two moves ahead rather than reacting to just the current board

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play 2048?

Use the arrow keys, or swipe on mobile, to slide every tile on the board in one direction. Tiles with the same number merge into one tile with double the value.

What is the goal of the game?

The traditional goal is to create a tile worth 2048, but you can keep playing after that to push your score even higher.

What happens when the board fills up?

The game ends once the board is full and no adjacent tiles share the same value, meaning no move can create a merge.

Is my best score saved?

Yes, your best score is stored locally in your browser so it stays available the next time you visit, without any account or sign-up.
