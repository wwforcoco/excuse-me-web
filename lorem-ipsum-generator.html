Lorem Ipsum Generator

Generate placeholder text for mockups, layouts, and designs.

About This Tool

This tool produces classic Lorem Ipsum-style placeholder text, generated word by word from the traditional Latin-derived word list, so you can fill layouts before real copy is ready.

How It Works

  1. Choose whether to generate by paragraphs, sentences, or words.
  2. Set the amount you need.
  3. Optionally start with the classic "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet" opener.
  4. Click Generate Text and copy the result into your design or document.

What This Tool Covers

  • Generate by paragraphs, sentences, or individual words
  • Up to 50 units at a time
  • Optional classic opening line for authenticity
  • One-click copy of the full result

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lorem Ipsum used for?

It's standard placeholder text used in design and publishing to show how a layout looks with body copy before final content is written.

Does the text mean anything?

No, it's scrambled Latin-derived words used purely for their visual text length and rhythm, not for meaning.

Can I generate just a few words instead of full paragraphs?

Yes, switch the "Generate by" option to Words and set your desired count.

Is this safe to paste into a live website by mistake?

It's meant only as a placeholder, so it should be replaced with real content before publishing.
