Frequently Asked Questions

How many milliliters are in a US cup?

One US cup equals approximately 236.588 milliliters.

Is a US gallon the same as a UK gallon?

No. A US gallon is 3.785411784 liters, while a UK (imperial) gallon is 4.54609 liters — both are included separately in this converter.

Can I convert tablespoons to milliliters for a recipe?

Yes, select tablespoon as the source unit and milliliter as the target for an instant, accurate result.

Does this converter use US or metric cup sizes?

It uses the US customary cup (236.588 ml). Note that some countries define a 'cup' slightly differently.

Can I convert cubic meters to liters?

Yes, cubic meters are included and convert directly to liters and every other volume unit on the list.

Do I need to create an account to use this?

No, it's free with no sign-up or account required.
