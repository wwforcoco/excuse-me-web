Image Color Picker

Click any spot on a picture to read its exact color value.

Drop a picture here, or click to choose one
Then click anywhere on it to read a color

What This Tool Does

Every pixel in a digital picture is a specific color. This tool reads the pixel under your cursor when you click on the canvas and reports it as a HEX code, RGB values, and HSL values, so you can match colors for design work.

Steps to Pick a Color

  1. Load a picture into the box above
  2. Move your mouse over the picture to see a live preview of the color under the cursor
  3. Click to lock in a color and add it to your saved swatches
  4. Click any saved swatch to copy its HEX code

What You Get

  • HEX, RGB, and HSL values shown for every picked pixel
  • A running list of saved swatches from the same picture
  • One-click copy of any color code to your clipboard
  • A live color preview that follows your cursor before you click
  • No part of the picture is uploaded to a server

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the color look slightly different from what I expected?

Zoomed-out previews can blend nearby pixels visually, but the tool always reads the exact underlying pixel value, not a blended average.

Can I pick colors from a photo, not just a graphic?

Yes, this works on any picture, including photographs, screenshots, and illustrations.

What is the difference between HEX, RGB, and HSL?

HEX and RGB both describe a color using red, green, and blue amounts, just in different notations. HSL describes the same color using hue, saturation, and lightness instead.

How many colors can I save?

There is no fixed limit. Each click adds another swatch to the list for that session.

Do the swatches stay after I close the tab?

No, the swatch list is only kept in memory for the current session and clears when you leave or reload the page.
