Frequently Asked Questions

How many psi are in a bar?

One bar equals approximately 14.5038 psi.

What is standard atmospheric pressure in pascals?

Standard atmospheric pressure is 101,325 pascals, equal to exactly 1 atmosphere.

What's the difference between mmHg and torr?

They're nearly identical: 1 torr is defined as 1/760 of an atmosphere, which is extremely close to 1 mmHg; both are included as separate options for convenience.

Can I convert tire pressure from psi to bar?

Yes, select psi as the source unit and bar as the target for an instant, accurate conversion.

Is this useful for medical blood pressure readings?

Blood pressure is typically read in mmHg, which is included here, though this general converter isn't a substitute for medical equipment or advice.

Is this converter free to use?

Yes, completely free with no sign-up or account required.
