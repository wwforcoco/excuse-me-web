Frequently Asked Questions

How many feet are in a meter?

One meter equals approximately 3.28084 feet. Enter any value in the converter to see the exact result.

Does this converter support nautical miles?

Yes, nautical miles are included alongside standard metric and imperial length units.

What's the difference between a mile and a nautical mile?

A statute mile is 1,609.344 meters, while a nautical mile is 1,852 meters; they're used in different contexts (land travel vs. sea and air navigation).

Can I convert inches to centimeters?

Yes, select inches as the 'from' unit and centimeters as the 'to' unit for an instant result.

Is there a limit to how large or small a number I can enter?

No practical limit — the converter handles very small and very large values, displaying results in scientific notation when needed.

Is this converter free to use?

Yes, completely free with no sign-up, account, or payment required.
