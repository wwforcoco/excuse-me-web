Random Decision Generator

Can't decide? Let this tool make the call, or pick randomly from your own options.

Click to decide

About This Tool

This tool gives you a random yes or no answer instantly, or picks one option at random from your own list when you can't decide between choices.

How It Works

  1. Click Yes or No for an instant random answer.
  2. Or type your own options, one per line, in the box below.
  3. Click Pick One For Me to get a random pick from your list.
  4. Run it again any time for a fresh decision.

What This Tool Covers

  • Instant random yes or no answers
  • Custom option list with random selection
  • Works for any decision, big or small
  • No limit on how many times you can try again

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the yes/no answer truly 50/50?

Yes, each click has an equal chance of returning yes or no.

Can I add more than a few options to pick from?

Yes, add as many lines as you like and one will be chosen at random.

What's this useful for?

Deciding where to eat, which task to do first, or settling a lighthearted disagreement.

Does it remember past decisions?

No, every click is a fresh, independent random pick.
