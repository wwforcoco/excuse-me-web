What Is a Resume Template?

A resume is a one-to-two page summary of your work history, skills, and education, written to get you an interview for a specific job. This template uses a simple, applicant-tracking-friendly layout: a header with your contact details, then Summary, Experience, Education, and Skills sections in that order.

How to Use This Template

  1. Fill in the fields on the left — your entries appear in the live preview.
  2. Click "Update Preview" any time you change a field.
  3. Click directly inside the preview to fine-tune wording, spacing, or line breaks.
  4. Download as a text file, or use Print → Save as PDF for a polished document.

Tips

  • Lead your summary with your job title and years of experience, not a generic statement.
  • List experience in reverse-chronological order — most recent job first.
  • Use one line per bullet point and start each with an action verb (led, built, managed).
  • Keep it to one page unless you have 10+ years of relevant experience.
  • Tailor your skills list to the keywords in the job posting.
