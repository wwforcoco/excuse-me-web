Solitaire

Classic Klondike Solitaire — sort a shuffled deck into four foundations from Ace to King.

↻

Tap a card to select it, then tap a destination pile to move it. Tap a card twice to send it to a foundation automatically.

What Is Solitaire?

This is the classic Klondike Solitaire card game, played with a single 52-card deck dealt across seven tableau columns. The goal is to sort every card into four foundation piles, one per suit, built up in order from Ace to King.

How to Play

  • Tap the stock pile to flip a new card into the waste pile
  • Build tableau columns downward in alternating colors, for example a red 7 on a black 8
  • Select a card, then tap a foundation or tableau column to move it there if the move is legal
  • Move every card into the four foundation piles, sorted by suit from Ace to King, to win

Strategy Tips

  • Uncover face-down tableau cards early instead of only chasing foundation moves
  • Empty a column when you can, since only a King can start a new one
  • Cycle through the stock pile methodically rather than randomly to track what's left
  • Use Auto-Complete once the tableau is fully sorted to finish the game quickly

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I move cards?

Tap or click a card to select it, then tap the pile or column you want to move it to. Selecting the same card again deselects it.

How do foundations work?

Each foundation pile builds up one suit starting from the Ace and continuing in order to the King. Double-tap a card to send it to its foundation automatically when it fits.

What can I place on an empty column?

Only a King, or a valid stack starting with a King, can be placed on an empty tableau column.

What does Auto-Complete do?

Auto-Complete repeatedly moves any available cards from the waste pile and tableau onto the foundations until no further automatic moves are possible.
