Frequently Asked Questions

Is a food Calorie the same as a calorie?

A food 'Calorie' (capital C) is actually a kilocalorie, equal to 1,000 small calories. This converter's 'kilocalorie' option matches food-label Calories.

How many joules are in a kilocalorie?

One kilocalorie equals 4,184 joules.

How do I convert my electricity bill's kWh to joules?

Select kilowatt-hour as the source unit and joule as the target; 1 kWh equals 3,600,000 joules.

What is a BTU used for?

BTU (British Thermal Unit) commonly measures heating and cooling capacity, such as air conditioner or furnace output.

Can I convert watt-hours to kilowatt-hours?

Yes, select watt-hour as the source and kilowatt-hour as the target for an instant conversion.
