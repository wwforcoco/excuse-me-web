Team Generator

Paste a list of names and split them into random, balanced teams.

About This Tool

This tool shuffles a list of names and deals them evenly into the number of teams you choose, useful for sports, classroom groups, or game nights.

How It Works

  1. Paste or type each name on its own line.
  2. Set how many teams you want.
  3. Click Generate Teams.
  4. Reshuffle any time for a new random split.

What This Tool Covers

  • Even distribution across 2 to 10 teams
  • Fair random shuffling of the full name list
  • Works with any group size
  • Instant re-shuffle with one click

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if the names don't divide evenly?

Extra names are distributed one at a time across teams, so team sizes differ by at most one person.

Can I use this for classroom groups?

Yes, it's commonly used for splitting students, coworkers, or players into random groups.

Does it remember previous team splits?

No, each click generates a brand new random split, and nothing is saved.

Is there a limit on how many names I can enter?

There's no hard limit, though very long lists may take a moment longer to display.
